About me

I am now a master in Software CollegeNortheastern University (NEU), under the supervision of Prof.Guibing Guo and co-supervision of Prof.Zhu Sun. Before that, I obtained my B.Eng degree from Information Science and Engineering College in YanShan University (YSU), 2018.

Research interest

My research topics include

  • Recommender System (e.g. sequential recommendation, session-based recommendation, etc.)

  • Data mining (e.g. information retrival)

  • Machine Learning techniques (e.g. deep learning)

But my research interests are not limited to the topics above, I am also curious about human language (e.g. question answering, dialogue generation, etc.) and interpretable machine learning.
